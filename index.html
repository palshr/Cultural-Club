CULTURAL CLUB IIIT BHOPAL

Cultural club is primarily responsible for all the very different cultural events that take place in the institution all round the year. The club comprises various sections that handle different types of cultural events. As a society, the club is focused on helping students explore their talents and hone them so as to enable success in different fields, helping them achieve the much needed balance between academics and extracurricular activities.
Focused on bringing out the hidden talents in our students, this club organizes multiple events regularly across many different fields of art and culture. Whether students wish to showcase their talents or try their hands at something new and discover their potential or passions, this is the place to be.
